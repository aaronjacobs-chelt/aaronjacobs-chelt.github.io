üçÑ Mycology & Mushroom Cultivation

My journey into mycology began five years ago when I discovered the fascinating world of fungi. I now run HappyCaps Mushroom Farm, where I cultivate various gourmet and medicinal mushrooms including:

  • Lion's Mane (Hericium erinaceus) - Known for its cognitive benefits
  • Blue Oyster (Pleurotus ostreatus var. columbinus) - A culinary delight with a mild flavor
  • Reishi (Ganoderma lucidum) - Prized for its adaptogenic properties
  • King Oyster (Pleurotus eryngii) - Meaty texture perfect for vegan dishes

I've developed my own substrate mixes and cultivation techniques, and I enjoy experimenting with different growing conditions to optimize yields and mushroom quality.
